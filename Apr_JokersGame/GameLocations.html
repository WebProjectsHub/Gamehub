The Police Station

The Police Station, Early Morning

The rain taps against the windows of the precinct. Detective Evelyn Carter sips her coffee, the weight of a long night lingering on her shoulders. A sudden flash on her monitor catches her attention — an encrypted internal message, no sender, no trace.

SUBJECT: “Game Start”
MESSAGE: “Detectives, the city is a board. The pieces are in place. Catch me if you can. If you want to play... go to Claybourne Lofts – Apartment 306. Bring your team.”

— J

Evie’s eyes narrow. She grips her coffee, her thoughts already racing.

The Police Station, Briefing Room

Evie assembles her team: Samantha Vaughn, Rachel Morales, and Lily. The atmosphere is tense.

  • Evie: "We’ve got a situation. No caller. No voice. Just this."
  • Samantha: "Encrypted?"
  • Evie: "Military-grade. Whoever sent this knows how to cover their tracks."
  • Lily (grinning): "A challenge?"
  • Rachel (frowning): "Or a trap."

They gear up quickly. No body, no immediate crime report—just an address and a cryptic message.

Location 02

Riverside Park (Underpass)



Crime Scene Clues

  1. Text graffiti.
    • Caesar cipher.
    • Will lead to false location.
    • Includes the word "footnote"
    • Will lead to a small graffiti on the bottom right of wall. The footnote will show the next crime location.
  2. "Footnote graffiti"
    • Book cipher, but from torn newspaper draped over the face of the victim
    • Decoded: it will show address of next crime location.
  3. The Joker card #2. Red coloration on the edge. (Collect all to reveal the final target)
  4. Newspaper article of a police operation 5 years ago, where a bystander was accidentally shot dead. The author of the article is the victim.
    • Use footnote as key to decode book cipher from newspaper article
  5. Flickering light
    • Morse code in 1's and 0's
    • The binary code spells the name of the victim.
    • Lightbulb also has QR code link to website. The website is password protected.
  6. Press badge with 4 digit alpha-numeric character
    • Use the alpha-numeric character as password for website.
    • Website will say timer is for next victim.
