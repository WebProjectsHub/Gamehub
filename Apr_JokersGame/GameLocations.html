Location 01

Claybourne Lofts – Apartment 306

  • Real-World Counterpart: Tribeca Lofts, Manhattan

    Address: 50 Murray Street, New York, NY 10007

    Description: Located in the heart of Tribeca, this building offers spacious loft apartments with high ceilings and large windows, reminiscent of classic New York industrial conversions.
  • Detectives arrive to find signs of struggle, but no body - only a Joker playing card displayed on a cushioned wooden chair in the middle of the apartment.
  • The apartment belongs to Daniel Keller, a journalist.
  • The detectives collect and catalog the evidence and coroner's assessment.

Crime Scene Clues

  1. Notebook with barcode sticker at the back. Sticker is morse code for laptop password.
  2. Laptop. Password-protected.
  3. The Joker card #1. Red coloration on the edge. (Collect all to reveal the final target)
  4. Writing on the first page of the notebook: xxxxxxx
    • Vigenere code
    • Decoded message: password to audio file that will be found in the laptop.
  5. Several torn pieces of paper found in the trash bin beside the laptop table.
    • When pieced together and folded in the correct way, will reveal the key to decode symbols on books arranged in the bookcase.
    • Coded message similar to that from Dan Brown's The Lost Symbol
  6. Audio file from laptop.
    • Incomprehensible voice record.
    • Need to be played backwards to reveal clue.
    • Revealed clue: "More victims to come."
  7. Crime scene photo of book case. Several of the books were scrawled with strange symbols.
    • Coded message similar to that from Dan Brown's The Lost Symbol
    • Use clue from torn pieces of paper to decode the message.
    • Decoded message points to where the body is.
  8. A riddle on second page of notebook:
    • ---
    • Riddle will tell detectives to play audio backwards.
  9. ---

Objectives

  1. Unlock the laptop. When guessed correctly, will show prompt saying there is additional objective (Decode the audio file)
  2. Find location of the body.
  3. Identify clue from the Joker card.
  4. Decode audio file from the laptop.
