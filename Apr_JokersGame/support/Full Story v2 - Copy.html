Puzzle Craze: The Joker Killer’s Game

Story Structure Summary (By Location and Branch)

📍 Location 1: The Anonymous Tip

Puzzle Set 1

  • Detectives receive an anonymous call about a murder.
  • They arrive to find signs of a struggle, but no body—only a Joker playing card.
  • Clues at the scene point toward another location.

📍 Location 2: Where the Body Lies

Puzzle Set 2

  • Detectives find the body of investigative journalist Daniel Keller.
  • They receive crime scene data and digital evidence.
  • Clues hint at a cover-up and a future crime.

🔀 Branching Point: Did they solve Puzzle Set 2 in time?

🟩 Branch 1 – Victim Saved

📍 Location 3: Next Target Foiled

Puzzle Set 4

  • Victim is rescued. Detectives solve the next puzzle within 24 hours.
  • Clues include crime scene evidence and a misleading red herring.
  • The red herring later becomes a clue about the Joker Killer’s identity.

📍 Location 4: The Joker's Trap

Puzzle Set 5

  • They stake out the location—but it’s a misdirection.
  • A body is discovered 24 hours later with taunts from the Joker.
  • Joker promises to increase the pace of his crimes.

📍 Location 5: The Card

Puzzle Set 7

  • A new victim is found with only a single Joker card left behind.
  • The detectives must now make a difficult choice.

🟥 Branch 2 – Victim Not Saved

📍 Location 3: Crime Scene Aftermath

Puzzle Set 6

  • Detectives fail to save the victim.
  • They gather evidence at the crime scene.
  • This evidence only leads to the next crime.

📍 Location 5: The Card

Puzzle Set 7

  • The same card puzzle appears.
  • The branching decision still applies.

🔀 Final Branching Point: Players Must Choose

Option A: Split Up Option B: Stay Together
  • Cover more ground, but risk isolation.
  • One may be trapped or targeted.
  • Move as a unit for safety.
  • Risk missing divergent clues.
