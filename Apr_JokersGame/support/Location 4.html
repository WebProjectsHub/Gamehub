Location 4 – Abandoned Broadcast Station

Setting: A long-defunct public radio station, WZRD 91.7. Crumbling walls, broken equipment, and a looming signal tower. The Joker Killer has lured the detectives here with a chilling message: "Your captain watched from the tower. He’ll watch again."

Exterior – WZRD Radio Station – Dusk

The detectives arrive in silence. Ivy coils up the brick facade. A raven flaps from the tower. The wind hums through the rusting vents.

Evie:

“This is where he wants us. The place is metaphor—broadcasting silence.”

Lily:

“Creepy. There’s something poetic about it... or maybe that’s what he wants us to think.”

Rachel:

“There are wires exposed near the side entrance—rigged recently. This place is supposed to be dead.”

Samantha:

“I’m picking up interference… like someone’s still transmitting from inside.”

Interior – Recording Booths

Inside the control room, dust coats the consoles—except for one active microphone and a running cassette player. The static clears just enough to let a voice crawl through:

🎙️ "They called him a hero. They buried my truth in a broadcast no one aired. But tonight… the captain hears the final broadcast."

A red recording light flickers on. They're live.

Evie:

“We’re not just observers. This is the next performance.”

Evidence & Puzzle Clues

  • 🕰️ A broken clock stuck at 11:17 — matches the cassette's timestamp.
  • 📄 A PSA script with bolded letters in blood-red ink — likely a cipher.
  • 📸 A photo board showing days of surveillance on the detectives.
  • 📅 A log with key dates marked. April 10 is circled: Tomorrow.
  • 🧱 A chalkboard wall with the phrase:
    "The tower falls when no one listens."
    "Voice lost in static."
    "Truth jammed. Signal blocked. Silence achieved."

The Stakeout

The speaker crackles again. A live voice. Not a recording.

🎙️ "You came to stop the silence. But the broadcast’s already been scheduled."

Samantha:

“He's traced to a live transmitter—it's bouncing off police HQ's rooftop systems.”

Evie:

“He’s going after the Captain. During morning roll call.”

Lily:

“This wasn’t the crime scene. It was a dress rehearsal.”

Hidden Compartment Discovery

Behind a false wall: wires, schematics of HQ's radio system, a transmitter shell… and a Joker card:

"All towers fall. Yours next."

Scene End – Stakes Raised

  • 🧠 Joker Killer is infiltrating HQ systems remotely.
  • 📡 Scheduled broadcast: April 10, 7:00 AM.
  • 🎯 Target: The Captain.
  • ⌛ The detectives have less than 12 hours to stop it.
