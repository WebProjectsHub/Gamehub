🎭 Location 2: Observatory Theater

Setting: An abandoned observatory turned black-box theater—dusty, half-collapsed in places, once used for community plays and stargazing events. Moonlight filters in through a cracked glass dome overhead. Faded constellations still cling to the ceiling. The stage sits quiet… too quiet.

[Exterior – Night – Observatory Entrance]
Evie: “Why here?”
Samantha: “This place has been closed for years. No power, no security system. Perfect spot to leave something… or someone.”
Lily: “If I were a puzzle-maker, this is where I’d build my next act.”
[Interior – Stage Floor – Minutes Later]

Dust motes swirl in the flashlight beams as the team sweeps the building. Footsteps echo. The floorboards creak like they're whispering.

On stage sits a single wooden chair, draped in red velvet. A masquerade mask hangs off the back. Above, the overhead spotlight flickers to life. No one touched it.

Rachel: “Someone rigged this. That light doesn’t run on mains… it’s battery-powered.”
Sofia: “Check this out.”

A scrap of sheet music lies on the seat, marked with the words:

“She sings to forget. But remembers the screams.”
Samantha: “This is Carmen Vale. She used to perform here... and she was seeing Keller.”
Evie: “Was she involved with the investigation?”
Rachel: “No. Nothing on her. Maybe she didn’t even know what he was working on.”
[Backstage – Dressing Room Mirror]

A cracked makeup mirror is smeared with red lipstick, writing in reverse:

“Behind the false glass, her truth waits frozen.”
Lily: “Is this… literal?”

Behind the mirror is a hidden cubby, containing:

  • A burned photograph of Carmen and Keller
  • A small USB recorder
  • A guest list for the Heights Hotel Charity Gala

They play the recording. Carmen’s voice comes through, shaking:

“He was going to tell them everything. I told him it wasn’t safe. They said… they said if I speak, I’ll end up like him.”

In the background:

A male voice, distorted: “You’re just the echo now. No one hears an echo.”
Sofia: “He’s silencing them. One by one.”
Climactic Beat

Suddenly, the old projector hums to life. A faint image flickers across the dome ceiling: a vintage reel showing a masked performer bowing on stage as the words appear in scrawled, flashing letters:

ACT TWO BEGINS AT THE HEIGHTS.
THE FINAL BOW WAITS FOR THE MAN WHO LED THE PARADE.

🔎 Scene End – Clues Unlocked

  • Carmen Vale is possibly the next victim.
  • The Joker sees the events as a performance—and the detectives as his actors.
  • "The man who led the parade" = Captain Reyes (a subtle foreshadow).
  • The next location is the Heights Hotel Gala.
