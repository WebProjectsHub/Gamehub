Character - Detective Rachel

Rachel Morales – The Forensic Perfectionist

Background

Rachel Morales always knew she wanted to be in law enforcement, but she never envisioned herself chasing criminals with a gun in hand. Instead, she saw herself in the lab, meticulously analyzing fibers, blood spatter, and DNA. Science was her language, and forensics was her passion.

Raised in a working-class family, Rachel was the first in her family to go to college. She pursued biochemistry and forensic science, earning top honors. While interning at the police department’s forensic unit, she developed a reputation for noticing what others missed—a single hair, a microscopic tear in clothing, or an odd chemical compound. Her attention to detail led to multiple breakthroughs in cases, catching the eye of seasoned detectives.

Despite preferring the lab over the streets, Rachel chose to become a detective rather than stay behind a microscope. She wanted to see investigations through from crime scene to conviction, ensuring that no mistakes were made in the pursuit of justice.

Personality & Approach

  • Perfectionist & Methodical – Every detail matters. If it doesn’t fit, she won’t rest until she knows why.
  • Cautious & By-the-Book – She believes in procedure and hates taking unnecessary risks.
  • Logical & Analytical – She approaches cases like a puzzle, breaking them down piece by piece.
  • Empathetic but Detached – She cares about victims but doesn’t let emotions cloud her judgment.
  • Secretly Competitive – While she pretends she doesn’t care, she hates being wrong.

Relationship with the Team

Evelyn "Evie" Carter (Lead Detective): Rachel sees Evie as a role model. She admires her leadership but sometimes struggles with her gut-driven approach.

Samantha Vaughn (Rookie, Ex-Hacker): Their differences make them clash. Samantha rushes into things, while Rachel prefers a careful analysis. However, when they work together, they make an unstoppable team.

Captain Marcus Holloway: She respects his experience and values his approval. She often seeks his input when doubting herself.

How She Feels About the Joker Killer Case

To Rachel, this case is a test of precision. The Joker Killer isn’t just leaving riddles—he’s leaving forensic evidence woven into the puzzles. Every fiber, every bloodstain, every number has a pattern.

The challenge? Deciphering that pattern before more people die.

While Samantha sees the Joker Killer as a game to win, Rachel sees him as a mistake waiting to happen. No one is perfect. Not even a killer obsessed with logic. And when he makes that mistake, she’ll be the one to find it.

"The devil is in the details. And I never miss a detail."
